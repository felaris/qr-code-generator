How to Read or Write an NFC Card Online

  1. Open FastQR in Chrome on an Android phone with NFC turned on.
  2. Tap the NFC tab at the top of the tool.
  3. Choose Read Tag to scan any NFC tag, or Write Tag to program a blank or rewritable NFC card.
  4. Hold the NFC card to the back of your phone. FastQR reads or writes it in under a second.

Web NFC uses the browser's built-in NDEFReader API — your data never leaves your device.
